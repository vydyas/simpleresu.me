As a developer, your resume needs to highlight both your technical skills and your ability to solve problems. Here are the best resume templates tailored for developers.

## What Makes a Great Developer Resume?

### 1. Technical Skills Section

A prominent technical skills section is essential. Group skills by category:
- **Languages**: JavaScript, Python, TypeScript
- **Frameworks**: React, Next.js, Django
- **Tools**: Git, Docker, AWS

### 2. Project Showcase

Highlight your projects with:
- Brief descriptions
- Technologies used
- Links to GitHub or live demos
- Impact or results

### 3. Clean, Modern Design

Tech companies appreciate clean, modern designs that reflect attention to detail.

## Recommended Templates

### Modern Professional Template

### Minimalist Tech Template

Ideal for frontend developers who want to showcase:

### Executive Elite Template

Best for senior developers and tech leads:

## Tips for Developer Resumes

1. **Show Your Code**: Include GitHub links
2. **Quantify Impact**: "Reduced load time by 40%"
3. **Highlight Technologies**: Match job requirements
4. **Keep It Updated**: Add new skills and projects regularly
